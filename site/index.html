Welcome to Alaska Skydive Center

Alaska Skydive Center is nestled in the spectacular Matanuska Valley. Located at the Palmer Municipal Airport, every jump comes with exceptional scenery — glaciers, rugged peaks, and the kind of wide-open Alaska views visitors travel here for. We are a family-friendly operation and we hope you come fly with us.

We offer first-time tandem jumps, the Alaska Skydive Program for those working toward a license, and a welcoming community for experienced jumpers. Ask us about professional video and still photography for your tandem.
