Welcome to Alaska Skydive Center

Welcome to Alaska Skydive Center — the Last Frontier’s premier drop zone. Come experience the thrill of Alaska at altitude. We specialize in everything from a first tandem jump to accelerated free-fall training, both with the option of professional videography or still-frame photography. Experienced skydivers are also always welcome to join us for a jump with locals or solo. ASC is just a short distance from Anchorage, located in Palmer, AK in the heart of “The Valley” at the Palmer Municipal Airport.

Welcome to Alaska Skydive Center!

On behalf of our entire team, we’d like to personally welcome you to Alaska Skydive Center here in beautiful Palmer, Alaska. We are thrilled you’ve chosen to join us for an unforgettable adventure in the skies above the Mat-Su Valley.

Whether you’re here for your very first tandem jump, training to become a licensed skydiver, or returning to share the skies with us again, our mission is simple: to provide you with the safest, most exciting, and most breathtaking skydiving experience possible. There’s nothing quite like seeing the rugged peaks, winding rivers, and endless Alaskan wilderness from freefall, and we’re honored to share that with you.

Our instructors and staff are passionate about what they do, and we take pride in combining professionalism, safety, and fun every step of the way. This is more than just a skydive — it’s a memory you’ll carry with you for life. Thank you for trusting us to be part of your adventure.

Blue skies,
Shannon, Tara, and Jason
