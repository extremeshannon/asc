Your own parachute

Learn to skydive

AFF progression, coaching, and a community that wants you to succeed.

Ready to jump on your own? Our Accelerated Freefall (AFF) program pairs you with instructors in freefall while you learn stability, awareness, and emergency procedures on every jump.

Between jumps you will study, debrief video, and pack skills with coaches who know Alaska skies. Licensed fun jumpers are part of the same family — loads, mentoring, and scenery that never gets old.

For our full program overview, see Alaska Skydive Program (ASP).
