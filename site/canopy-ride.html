Under parachute

Canopy ride

Quiet air, big views, and a soft finish on the grass.

After freefall, the canopy ride is your chance to breathe, steer (if conditions allow), and take in the Mat-Su from a slower, peaceful vantage point.

Your instructor flies a predictable pattern toward our landing area. We coach you to lift your legs for landing so you finish standing or sliding in gently — then it is high-fives, photos, and the grin that does not quit.
