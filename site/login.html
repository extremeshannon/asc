Public site vs staff login

dev.alaskaskydivecenter.com is the Alaska Skydive Center marketing website (static pages, booking links). Staff and operations use MalfunctionDZ on a different hostname — not this domain.

If you expected the orange MalfunctionDZ login here, your server nginx is sending this hostname to the wrong app (:8000 instead of the ASC site on :8006). Fix the proxy_pass for dev.alaskaskydivecenter.com per infra/nginx/dev.alaskaskydivecenter.com.conf in the platform repo.

Open MalfunctionDZ sign in

Using a dev server? Try dev.malfunctiondz.com/login instead.
